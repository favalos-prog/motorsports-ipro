List of Objectives and ConstraintsOur objective is to create a battery pack with the energy required to finish the raceIn our first stages we are using Power to come up with a preliminary S&P configurationWe don’t know how much energy is requiredIt is the Data Generation Teams job to provide us with the voltage and current required to complete the raceOnce they give us those requirements we will verify that our inverters and motors can handle that voltage and currentWe will calculate energy required based off the voltage and current requirements from the Data Generation TeamOur final S&P configuration depends the energy requiredConstraints400 V is our voltage cap due to Inverter operating limit300 A is our current cap due to motor operating limit (150 for each motor)80 kW is our power cap due to competition regulations (40 kW for each motor)Peak singular motor power is 70 kW but can only be operated for a short burst of time.The motors may need a cooling system in order to operate with higher powerFor Continuous Motor Power: Air cooled -> 28 kW max/motor, Liquid cooled -> 30 kW max/motor, Combined air & liquid cooled -> 35 kW max/motorWe may not need a battery pack capable of 80 kW max powerSpecs from last years carPack Volume (just cells)= 2698 in^3# of cells = 432Configuration = 108s4pPower = 81.6 kW (not accounting inverter losses)Max TS Voltage = 453.6 VNominal TS Voltage = 388.8 VCurrent = 180 A (60 A to each motor, they had 100 A fuses)Their motor (EMRAX 208) could operate at 40 kW continuous motor power with combined air & liquid cooling system
